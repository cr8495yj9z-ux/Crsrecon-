Casualty Reconstruction Systems

CRS Recon develops practical reconstruction tools and visualization systems for marine and transportation incident analysis. Our focus is clear, hands-on understanding of real-world scenarios through modular modeling, structured layouts, and professional presentation.

About CRS Recon

CRS Recon is focused on building clear, physical, and visual tools that help communicate sequence, configuration, movement, and incident context. The goal is straightforward: make reconstruction easier to understand, explain, and refine.
